Bitcoin: A Peer-to-Peer Electronic Cash System

By Satoshi Nakamoto

Introduction

▼
Bitcoin solves the double-spending problem in digital cash without requiring a trusted third party. It uses a peer-to-peer network and proof-of-work to maintain a public transaction history.

Digital Signatures

▼
Each coin is a chain of digital signatures. Owners transfer coins by digitally signing the previous transaction and the next owner's public key.

P2P Network

▼
Transactions are broadcast to all nodes. Nodes collect transactions into blocks and compete to solve a proof-of-work puzzle. The longest chain represents consensus.
